Welcome to this PWA Demo

The purpose of this Showroom is to demo some design works and under test features of a Progressive Web App Ionic/Angular embedded with many add-ons like NgRx, Material, Leaflet and much more.

It also includes a dashboard for a front fullstack api associated to a NodeJS/Koa/TypeORM backend Demo, called Koa

Don't hesitate to have a look around and keep in mind that this site is dynamic and adapted according to daily needs of his owner.
